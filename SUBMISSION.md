## 🎥 Demo video script (~75 seconds)

Record your screen at the live Space. Talk over it.

1. **(0–8s) The hook — it's already playing.** Open the Space. The knight film is mid-scene.
   > "This is LLM Cinema. A *small* language model just wrote, drew, and directed this entire
   > film — every character, the snow, the forest, the camera — in ASCII. No video. No sound."

2. **(8–25s) It's a real model doing real work.** Go to **Make a film**, type something silly
   like `a raccoon who runs a midnight noodle stand`. Hit Make.
   > "Give it any idea and Cinema — a fine-tuned MiniCPM — writes a 13-beat story, draws the
   > cast, picks the setting and camera, and stages it live."
   Let a few shots play.

3. **(25–45s) The gallery — instant delight.** Open **Gallery**, click **The Dragon Baker**
   (films play the moment you pick them).
   > "It's genuinely *delightful* — a dragon who'd rather bake than fight, defending her
   > bakery with an oven. Seven hand-picked films, all replay instantly."

4. **(45–65s) You're in the story.** Open **Adventure**, type `a lighthouse keeper hears
   singing from the sea`, press Begin, then click a choice.
   > "And it branches — choose-your-own-adventure. You pick what happens next, and it continues
   > your story."

5. **(65–75s) Close + share.** Hit **Download this film (GIF)**.
   > "Small model, big imagination. Make your own and share it. LLM Cinema."

**Tips:** keep the endpoint warm before recording (open the Space, run one Make). If a live
generation is slow on camera, cut to the Gallery (instant) — never show a spinner.

## 🏆 Why this wins (talking points for judges)

  there's nothing. Not a wrapper.

- **Delightful + original:** inline ASCII cinema + interactive adventures. Nobody else has this.
- **Polished + reliable:** auto-plays on load, gallery never needs the model, one-click GIF share.
